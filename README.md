Overview

This is a text-based or simple UI implementation of the classic casino game, Blackjack (also known as 21). The objective is to beat the dealer's hand without exceeding a total of 21.

Features

1).Player Initialization: Prompts users for their name and starting amount of chips.

2).Core Gameplay: Supports fundamental Blackjack mechanics (Hit/Stand).

3).Score Tracking: Displays current cards, the sum total, and player's chip balance (e.g., "Jack: $0607").

4).Game State Management: Includes a "Start Game" button/option to begin a new round.

How to Play

1).Launch the game: Run the application.

2).Enter Details: Follow the prompts to input your player name and the amount of chips you want to start with.

3).Start Round: Click the "Start Game" button or follow the on-screen instructions to deal the initial hands.

4).Hit or Stand: Decide whether to "draw a new card" (hit) to increase your sum or stand with your current total.

5).Win or Lose: The game determines the winner based on standard Blackjack rules and updates your chip count.
